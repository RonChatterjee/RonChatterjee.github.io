About us

Quizinc was started by two students Harish Mohan and Anup Nair in 2003. Since its inception it has become one of the premier college quizzing clubs in India.

  • Mission - Here at Quiz Inc. we believe that quizzing is not all about being all-knowing. We believe that it is about knowing our surroundings and applying basic deductive powers to relate them and form a meaningful answer.
